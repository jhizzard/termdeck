### [T1] FINDING 2026-06-12 13:06 ET — PKCE is hard-required by SDK zod schemas at BOTH endpoints; no relaxation switch exists; decision = per-client opt-in `allow_no_pkce`, confidential-static-only — ⚠️ ORCH/T4 VETO POINT
Empirical determination (the brief's known-trap, answered from SDK source):
- `/authorize`: `RequestAuthorizationParamsSchema` (handlers/authorize.js:77-84) requires `code_challenge: z.string()` AND `code_challenge_method: z.literal('S256')`. A static client may be explicitly configured `allow_no_pkce: true` (env: `TERMDECK_BRIDGE_STATIC_CLIENT_ALLOW_NO_PKCE=1`).
